The goal is to get the least sum of cards among all the players on the board.
Ace is valued a point, Joker is 0 and red King is -1, rest of the cards are valued at their printed value.

Some cards have a special "ability" when revealed,
the game will instruct you to use it when revealed.

Any player can call CABO on any of his/hers turns.

Don't rush, take your time, make sure you know which cards you are holding.

Have fun!
